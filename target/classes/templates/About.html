About Our Smart Village

The Smart Village Management System (SVMS) is an innovative platform designed to transform rural areas into self-sustaining, technologically advanced communities. By integrating modern technologies such as IoT, AI, and cloud computing, the SVMS aims to improve the quality of life for residents, enhance local governance, and promote sustainable development.

By addressing key areas such as healthcare, education, agriculture, and governance, the SVMS aims to bridge the urban-rural divide and foster inclusive growth.Easy access to government services such as birth certificates, land records, and pensions through a unified digital portal.
